The Wumpus World

What is the Wumpus world?

The Wumpus world is a simple world used to demonstrate the powers and behaviors of knowledge-based agents. A Wumpus world is a cave, represented by a square matrix, in which agents must avoid monsters and deadly obstacles in their pursuit of gold

Our Wumpus world implementation

During the Fall of 2021, my team and I collaborated to create this project for a course at Montana State University: CSCI 446 Artificial Intelligence. Our solution was developed in Python and implemented two types of agents: one that used first order logic and a knowledge base and one that was a simple reactionary agent.

Results
