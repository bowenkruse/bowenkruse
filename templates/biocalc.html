bioCalc

What is bioCalc?

bioCalc is the implementation of some of the most common data-structures and algorithms in bioinformatics including Z, suffix trees, KMP, Needleman-Wunsch, and Smith-Waterman

The Z algorithm:

The Z algorithm finds all occurrences of a pattern in a text in LINEAR TIME! This of course is thus very useful in the world of bioinformatics. To the right, simply enter in your string, and pattern that you wish to find within it.
